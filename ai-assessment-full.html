AI Future Star™ Assessment

Step 1 – Organization Information

Confirm who is being assessed, the scope, and the assessor before scoring begins.

Step 2 – Path 1: AI Maturity Assessment

Score each sub-indicator 0–100 and assign an Evidence Maturity Rating (EMR). Dimension scores update live as you type.

Step 3 – Path 2: AI Capability Assessment

Score each capability floor's indicators. A floor's score is the average of its sub-indicators.

Step 4 – Reliability Assessment (AI Trust Score)

Score Accuracy, Safety, Security, and Explainability for each floor. A floor only passes its gate if Capability ≥ 80 AND this Reliability score meets the required threshold.

Step 5 – Evidence Register

Review the supporting evidence behind the scores entered. Evidence coverage feeds into Assessment Confidence.
